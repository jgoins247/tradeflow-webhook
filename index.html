CallCovered is an AI-powered phone answering service operated by James Goins, sole proprietor, based in Tennessee, USA. Contact: support@callcovered.io

2. Information We Collect

When a caller contacts a business using CallCovered, we may collect:

  • Caller's name and phone number
  • Address or location of the job site
  • Description of the service needed
  • Call recording and transcript

Business owners who subscribe to CallCovered provide their name, phone number, email address, and business information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected during calls is used solely to:

  • Notify the business owner of the incoming lead via SMS
  • Send the caller a confirmation that their message was received
  • Facilitate appointment scheduling when requested

4. SMS Messaging

By calling a business that uses CallCovered, callers consent to receive a one-time SMS confirmation message. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out of future messages. Reply HELP for help.

We do not share, sell, or rent mobile phone numbers or any personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

5. Data Retention

Call data is retained for up to 30 days for operational purposes and then deleted.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support@callcovered.io.
